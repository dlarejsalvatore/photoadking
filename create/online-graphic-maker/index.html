FAQs - Online Graphic Maker

  • How can I make a stunning design?

    • You don't need to be a graphic designer to create graphic design for advertising or social media marketing. Just use ready-made templates provided by PhotoADKing OR create your own.

    PhotoADKing provides quick, simple and easy to use graphic editor to customize. Add text, stickers, backgrounds from graphic collection or add your own creatives.

  • How can I use of 3D editor in graphic design?

    • Creating graphic design and you think of 2D imagery. But moving into the third dimension can really help your designs to stand out.

    PhotoADKing provides stunning 3D editor to create 3D objects, 3D shapes, and 3D text arts which can be used later to highlight most important important information in your design.

  • Can I make social media promotional graphics?

    • Yes, You can. With PhotoADKing's graphic maker you can create graphic design for any social media channel which includes Instagram post, Instagram story, Facebook post, Facebook cover, YouTube thumbnail, YouTube banner and more.

    Just edit ready-made design templates created by professional designer team of PhotoADKing.

  • Can I create a design with custom dimensions?

    • Yes, Custom dimensions let you create a design in any size. To create a design with custom dimensions,

    - Click Create New option from the top panel
    - Click Custom Size
    - Enter the preferred dimensions in the width and height field
    - Indicate if the dimensions you entered are in pixels, centimeters, inches, or millimeters by clicking the drop-down menu
    - Click on create option to open the editor with your custom-sized canvas

    Tip: Height should be greater than width for portrait sizes, while width should be greater than height for landscape.
