FAQs - Video Templates

  • Can I change the background video from templates?

    • Yes, You can. Click on add video option from the left side panel. Choose a video to upload and click open. Click on a video to add them to your design.

  • How can I trim my videos?

    • Click on the video you want to edit, Click on the editor panel from the right side panel, Click on trim video, Drag to blue slider on the video and select the duration you'd like to keep or you may also select the duration of video by using up-down arrows in the start time & end time, Click on done to save.

  • Can I add/change the music to video templates?

    • Yes, You can. Click on the add audio option from the left side panel. Click on upload. Click on an added audio to add them to your design.
