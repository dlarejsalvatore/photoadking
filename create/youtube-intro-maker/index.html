FAQs - Youtube Intro Maker

  • How long should an intro be?

    • An intro video should be short, around 10 seconds. it is an ideal length of an intro video out there. If the intro video is Longer than 10 seconds it will result in losing valuable viewers. If the video is short then you don't have time to make an impression.

  • Can I add music to my intro video?

    • Yes, You can definitely add your own music to your intro video. Go to edit music and then click on replace, then upload a custom track you’ve created and you are good to go.

  • What to say in an intro video?

    • Your intro video should include you and your product or service in a way that feels authentic. You can tell a story or add one or two lines that would help your audience to understand who you are and what you do.

  • Can I upload my own logo into the intro maker?

    • Yes, You can upload your own logo into our video maker. Go to the edit image option on the left side of the editor then click on replace then upload your logo. If you want to change your template you can just go to the left side and select templates, We have a bunch of new designs waiting for you to take advantage of.

  • What if I can’t find a template I like?

    • Don't worry, We have a huge selection of video intro templates in the making, but just in case if you didn't find any templates you can always make it from a blank canvas.

  • Is it easy to download the intro video when I’m done?

    • Yes! You can download your intro video when you are done editing it. If you want a high-quality video then you need to have a pro plane.
