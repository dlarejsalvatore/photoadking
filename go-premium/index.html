Frequently asked questions

Need some answers? Here's all you want to know about PhotoADKing.

  • How long can I use the free plan?
    • You can use the free plan as long as you like. Still, you can only create and edit 10 designs. The free limit is designed so that everyone can try PhotoAdKing before buying. We believe that all high-quality products come at a cost, PhotoAdKing makes no exception.
  • Why should I upgrade from the free plan?
    • Upgrading from a free plan will allow you to create more than 10 designs and will remove the watermark & Ads. Premium users also get unlimited access to our stock photo collection, premium templates, organizing features and advanced 3D editor as long as their subscription is active.
  • Will my subscription renew automatically?
    • Yes, if you don't cancel the auto-renewal, you will be charged on a monthly or yearly basis depending on your subscription type. If you only need it for one month you can make the purchase then cancel the auto-renewal from your billing profile.
  • What happens with my ongoing paid subscription, when I upgrade to another premium plan?

    • Our system will provide you an additional number of days pending in existing subscription to the new subscription.

    It will show you the exact information when you will try to upgrade it.

  • Can I cancel my subscription?

    • Yes, you can cancel at any time. You can keep using your current paid plan until it ends. However, we are not offering prorated refunds.

  • Why my payment failed?

    • Your payment would fail for many reasons, and the most common reasons include:

    • Incorrect PayPal account.
    • An insufficient balance of funds in your PayPal account.
    • The credit card has expired.
    • Payment rejected or delayed by your bank.
    • Please check your payment information and try to resubscribe again.
    • Also, contact our support team so we can help you.
  • Can I change my subscription plan?

    • Sorry, currently we don't support you changing your subscription plan directly. If you want to change, please choose "Cancel My Subscription" on your account page and then resubscribe for another plan.

    Our system will provide you an additional number of days pending in existing subscription to the new subscription.

    It will show you the exact information when you will try to upgrade it.
